The FGY Experience

FGY art gallery, based on the Venerable Grand Master Hsing Yun's dedication to build a Pure Land through Humanistic Buddhism, is an ecpression of virure, compassion and wisdom. As the virtues and produndity of life can be reflected in art, the gallery aims to encourage an appreciation of traditional and contemporary concepts of Easterm philosophy and culture.
Set in the heart of Melbourne, the gallery transposes art and cultural appreciation into a whole new experience as it offers a tranquil oasis from the noise and stress of today's city enviroment. There is an Exhibition Room, a Tea House and Meditaion Room within the building. Apart from viewing the series of world-class exhibitions, you can take the opportunity to enjoy the diverse vegetarian menu and special blends of tea offered at the Tea House. Or simply drop in the meditate or relax in the Meditation Hall.
Our entering the building, serenity and calm energy can be immediately felt. FGY Art Gallery offers a genuine insight into the colouful Eastern bulture of the past and present.

Meditation
in the Hall

A quiet room is designed for inner reflection and contemplation.

Located upstairs away from the Exhibition Room and Tea House, the highlight of the Meditation Hall is the traditional Buddhist shrine. The Hall allows visitors the opportunity for prayer and light offering or simply to enter into the timeless space of meditation.

Stillness pervades the area, ideal for inner reflection and contemplation.

Soft lighting, subtle incense and provision of comfortable medication mats create a conductive atmosphere for meditation.

Meditation sessions during lunch hours are conducted for the benefit of people who work around the city.
