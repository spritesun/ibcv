Introduction Fo Guang Chinese was founded in 1995 to achieve Master Hsing Yun's concept of fostering talents through education; and to carry forward traditional chinese culture, to teach moral ethics, to enhance student's ability to listen, speak and write the Chinses language and to develop a balance between knowledge is Venerable Miao Yung. At present, we have several classes which include; Kindergarten, VELS levels 1 to 6 and VCE. We also have after school tuition classes and activities in the afternoon. Also the Box Hill branch was established two years ago. At the moment there are two classes running at the Box Hill Branch.
